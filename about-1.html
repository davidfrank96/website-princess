About Princess Luxury Hotels

Princess Luxury Hotels and Tourism has been elegantly crafted and prearranged to achieve a measure of comfortable luxury to satisfy the senses of every guest from all over the world. Ideally situated in a quiet area of the city of Ilorin, Nigeria. The hotel radiates glorious beauty of contemporary decor perfectly blended with the exotic warmth of unparalleled hospitality.

Princess Luxury Hotels has the friendly and relaxing hotel accommodation you need and has been home to the great and the good, the intellectual elite, the cream de la cream and thousands of discerning guests.

Whether you are off on a vacation or on a business trip, you can always make use of its excellent services and attractive rooms. For a truly unforgettable experience, in lavish comfort and uninterrupted privacy, fully air conditional rooms, 24 hours power supply, over 10 channels satellite TV, free wifi hotspot for our guest, free airport pickup and drop off.

Experience the ultimate comfort and pleasure at Princess Luxury.

Dr. Sola Managing Director
